Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Kharcha Saathi ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal expense tracking application. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Kharcha Saathi, we collect:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google.
  • Expense data: Expenses, budgets, categories, and recurring transactions that you create within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the expense tracking service
  • Display your expenses, budgets, and reports
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the service.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on cloud infrastructure). We use row-level security (RLS) to ensure that only you can access your own data. All connections use HTTPS encryption.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication (sign-in). Google receives your authentication request but not your expense data.
  • Supabase: For database hosting and authentication.
  • Vercel: For application hosting.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your expense data at any time from within the app.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your stored data
  • Export your data from the app
  • Delete your data
  • Request account deletion by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

Kharcha Saathi is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please reach out via the app or visit kharcha-saathi.vercel.app.
